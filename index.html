Pig Game

In pig game, two players race to collect 100 points before the other one. Each player rolls a dice. If the dice is 1, the player forfeits their turn, loses their points for the round and the other player plays instead. If the dice is not 1, the player can either continue playing (until dice == 1), or they can hold their points, by adding them to their total score and giving up their turn.

🔄 New game: Starts game with Player 1 default.

🎲 Roll dice: Generates random roll of dice.

📥 Hold: Adds points to total.
